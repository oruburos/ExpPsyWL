<div ng-show="guidedStep == 1">
<p><b>Starting Information</b></p>
<p>Enter your starting information, including your weight, sex, age, height, and physical activity level. </p>
<br />
<p><b>Physical Activity Level</b></p>
<p>Click the "Estimate Your Level" button to find your physical activity level. </p>
<p>Typical physical activity level numbers range from 1.4 (sedentary) to 2.5 (very active).</p>
<p>The default value of 1.6 describes someone who does very light activity at school or work (mostly sitting) and moderate physical activity (such as walking or cycling) at least once a week.</p>
<br />
<p><b>Body Weight Planner How-to Video</b></p>
<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v1gluQwieog" target="_blank">Watch a video</a>
<a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/disclaimers" target="_blank" class="eld i-exit" aria-label="External link" title="View NIDDK's External Link Disclaimer"></a>
to see how to use the Body Weight Planner.</p>
</div>
<div ng-show="guidedStep == 2">
<p><b>Goal Weight</b></p>
<p>Enter your goal weight and when you would like to reach it.</p>
<p>You can enter a number of days OR choose a specific date using the calendar.</p>
</div>
<div ng-show="guidedStep == 3">
<p><b>Physical Activity Changes</b></p>
<p>Click the "Calculate" button to show how you will change your physical activity.</p>
<p>Changing your physical activity can help you reach and maintain your goal weight.</p>
<p>The physical activity changes you make will impact how many calories you need to eat to reach your goal and maintain it after you have reached it.</p>
<p>If you don’t want to change your physical activity, click the “Next Step” button to skip this step.</p>
</div>
<div ng-show="guidedStep == 4">
<p><b>Results</b></p>
<p>The "reach your goal" and "maintain your goal" calories displayed assumes you will continue the physical activity changes you previously entered in step 3.</p>
<p>Do your results seem too high? People often underestimate how much they eat and the Body Weight Planner values are accurate for most people. If your metabolism is abnormally low or you are very sedentary then Body Weight Planner values will be too high.</p>
<p><b>Want to Make a Change?</b></p>
<p>Use the Previous Step button to go back and change your physical activity or goal weight.</p>
<p><b>Tips for Eating Healthy</b></p>
<div>
<ul style="margin-left:20px;">
<li>Everything you eat and drink matters. The right mix can help you be healthier now and in the future. Small amounts of your favorite high-calorie foods may be part of your weight-loss plan as long as you burn more calories than you take in.</li>
<li>Additional resources:
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.choosemyplate.gov" target="_blank">ChooseMyPlate.gov</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/weight-management/just-enough-food-portions" target="_blank">Just Enough for You: About Food Portions</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/diet-nutrition/changing-habits-better-health" target="_blank">Changing Your Habits for Better Health</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Tips for Getting or Staying Active</b></p>
<div>
<ul style="margin-left:20px;">
<li>You don’t need to be active for long periods to get the recommended amount of regular physical activity. Find ways to build short bursts of physical activity into your day, like taking a 10-minute walking break at work or using the stairs instead of an escalator or elevator.</li>
<li>Additional resources:
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/weight-management/tips-get-active" target="_blank">Tips to Help You Get Active</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/weight-management/walking-step-right-direction" target="_blank">Walking: A Step in the Right Direction</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/weight-management/staying-active-at-any-size" target="_blank">Staying Active at Any Size</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p><b>Learn more:</b> Visit our
<a href="https://www.niddk.nih.gov/health-information/weight-management/myths-nutrition-physical-activity" target="_blank">Myths about Nutrition &amp; Physical Activity</a>
page to learn more about common myths, facts, and tips.
</p>
